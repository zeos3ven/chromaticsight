about Space

We want to help transition to an open, trustless web.

Siloed -> User-controlled

Rethinking online ownership is key to the open web. Users should own their accounts, storage, data, and permission companies to use them as they (users) see fit. Not the other way around.

Abuse-prone -> Trustless

Security, privacy, and ownership can't depend on trust. Adopting trustless standards in storage, authentication, etc. is a must to ensure censorship and data abuse are not an option.

Centralized -> Decentralized

The web is currently held by a handful of centralized actors. We believe the future of the web is open and permissionless, users should't have to rely on a handful of organizations online.

To ensure new standards are adopted universally, we must maintain an open source ecosystem. The future web has to be built and reviewed in community.

A new trust(less) standard.

We're helping to make user-controlled, encrypted, peer-to-peer, and censorship resistant apps the new norm.

Great experiences can be user-controlled.

All product we know and love today can begin their road to trustlessness without sacrificing their experience. We're making file storage user-owned with Space to prove that.
